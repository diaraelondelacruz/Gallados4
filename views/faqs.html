Get in Touch with Us

You can book an event by contacting us through our website, Facebook page, or by calling us directly. We'll discuss your event details, date, and package preferences.

We cater to birthdays, debuts, weddings, christenings, kiddie parties, and other special celebrations. We tailor our services to fit your event needs.

Our packages vary depending on the type of event, number of guests, and inclusions. Please visit our Packages page or contact us for a detailed quote.

We recommend booking at least 1–3 months in advance to secure your preferred date. For peak seasons, booking earlier is highly advised.

We accept cash, GCash, and bank transfers. A down payment is required to confirm your booking, with the remaining balance due before or on the event day.

Our packages serve as a base, but we are open to customization. You may add or adjust inclusions based on your needs and budget.

Simply reach out to us via our contact page, Facebook, or phone. Our team will guide you through the booking process step by step.

Cancellations and rescheduling are subject to our terms and conditions. Please contact us as soon as possible so we can assist you accordingly.

We primarily serve within our local area, but we may accommodate events in nearby locations depending on availability. Please contact us to discuss the details.
